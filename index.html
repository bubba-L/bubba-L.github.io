Projects

UniTrade

Unitrade is a concept website developed as a class project, where our group was tasked with coming up with an original idea rather than creating a fully functional site. Designed as a platform for university students to buy and sell used school supplies, the website is purely interactive, allowing users to navigate pages and input text but without storing data or enabling actual transactions. Our team of four collaborated to ensure a cohesive design and user experience, with each member focusing on specific sections while providing mutual support and feedback. I contributed to the development of the start page, home page, and settings page, while my groupmates worked on other sections, with all of us reviewing each other's work to maintain consistency across the site.

Board of Community Assistance Project

Summer 2023

The Board of Community Assistance (BCA) is an organization in Upper Merion Township that was established in 2013 through a partnership with the Valley Forge Casino Resort. Its primary purpose was to provide financial assistance through grants to nonprofit organizations that benefited the residents of the township. The BCA also offered scholarships to high school students who planned to attend post-secondary education or training.

As part of my effort to give back to the community, I worked on a project to help close the "digital divide" in Upper Merion Township. This divide affected those who were unfamiliar with or anxious about using technology, often leading to barriers in accessing essential services and staying connected. To address this, I offered free technology help at the Community Center. I set up a help desk twice a week from May to August, providing one-on-one support for residents with common technology issues. Additionally, I created printed guides that addressed frequently asked questions and problems. This initiative aimed to ensure that everyone in the community, regardless of their comfort with technology, could navigate the increasingly digital world.
